Tones of Life



Tones of Life is an experimental music generator based upon Stephen Wolfram's elementary rules of cell automation. Press cells on the gameboard to make them 'live', and press the step button to see how each column in the grid comes alive according to the different rules you can select. You can also change the scale or the tempo (in beats per minute). Autoplay will continue looping through the board. See what sounds you can discover!



Built using Tone.js by Emily Intersimone at Grace Hopper Academy
